Gentle Hatha Yoga with GWNYS

GWNYS offers Gentle Hatha yoga, a gentle and meditative style of yoga which focuses on breathing, stretching, and body control. It is ideal for beginners, and particularly those who want to relax and disconnect from everyday stress.

Vinyasa Yoga with GWNYS

GWNYS offers Vinyasa Yoga, a fast-paced, fitness-based approach.

Restorative Yoga with GWNYS

GWNYS offers Restorative Yoga, a relaxing style of yoga that relives stress and heals.

Iyengar Yoga with GWNYS

GWNYS offers Iyengar Yoga, a precise type of yoga that focuses on balance and alignment. It strengthens ligaments and muscles.
